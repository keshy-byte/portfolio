At Agri Tech, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural sector through sustainable practices and innovative technologies. Founded in 2024 by Mary Wangechi Kimaru, a Software Engineer with a deep passion for environmental sustainability and technological advancement in farming, Agri Tech aims to empower farmers and agricultural enthusiasts to adopt eco-friendly practices that benefit both the planet and their yields. Driven by the challenges faced by the agricultural community, including climate change, soil degradation, and the need for higher productivity,I envisioned a platform that not only educates but also provides practical solutions. Agri Tech is the culmination of years of research, hands-on experience, and the belief that technology and nature can work together harmoniously. Our mission is to create a sustainable farming future by promoting practices that enhance soil health, conserve water, and reduce the reliance on chemical pesticides and fertilizers. Through our website, we share insights on organic farming, precision agriculture, and the latest in agritech innovations, from drone farming to smart irrigation systems. Join us on this journey towards a greener future.
